Top Three Activities To Do In Palm Springs

Pool Days

There are great pools around Palm Springs to cool down catch a tan.

Art Museum

You can find art all around town and at the Art Museum located in downtown. Every Thursday there is a street fair and between 5pm-7pm it is free admission to the art museum.

Night
Life

When the weather cools down in the evening, everyone comes out to play. Concerts in the park, happy hour, and plenty of bars and dancing.
